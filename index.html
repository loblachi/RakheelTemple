Shree Sanathan Mandir

🏛️ About our Temple

The shree Sanathan Mandir of umkomaas has been around from 2014. Our temple was initially established by the community and now has grown its size and members. We are the first Hindi temple in the Umkomaas area and through a lot of hard work and dedication from family friends and community members it has become a great place for people to come and perform their prayers and make their offerings. The temple is run by Pundit Sanjay Mudhai who is the resident officiating priest. Our temple is dedicated to Shree Hanuman and Durga maata.
